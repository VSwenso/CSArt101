Pseudocoding Rock-Paper-Sccissors:
1. player one picks between Rock Paper or Scissors
2. Player two picks between Rock Paper or Scissors
3. on the count of three the players reveal their shapes.
4. Rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock
5. If both players make the same shape, it is a tie and the game is repeated or played again.
